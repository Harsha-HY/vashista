Fee Structure (2026 - 2027)

Annual fee covers tuition, smart classes, co-curricular training, and basic laboratory maintenance.

Grade / Class Tuition Fee (Annual) Lab & Computer Fee Payment Terms
Nursery / LKG / UKG Contact Administration Nil 3 Term Installments
Class I - V Contact Administration Included in Tuition 3 Term Installments
Class VI - VIII Contact Administration Nominal charges apply 3 Term Installments
Class IX - X Contact Administration Applicable for Lab Exams 3 Term Installments

Important Notice

Fees are divided into 3 equal terms, payable in June, October, and January. Late payment fees and administrative penalties are applicable post the due dates. We follow the Telangana State Board of Secondary Education (SSC) curriculum. To prepare students for future global challenges, we integrate CBSE reference guides, smart visual learning systems, and advanced computer training lessons into our daily routine.
Student safety is our top priority. Our entire school building is under active CCTV surveillance, and fire safety systems are installed on each floor. Regular safety drills are conducted. Additionally, we have separate toilets for boys and girls, pure drinking RO water facilities, and a dedicated sick bay for first-aid emergencies.
We utilize a digital Campus Management System (CMS). Parents receive separate login credentials to track attendance, monthly class performance, progress reports, and notices. We also hold mandatory Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTMs) on the second Saturday of every month to discuss student progress in detail.
